Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our performance ecosystem.

How do I create my first event orchestration? expand_more
Simply navigate to the 'Dashboard' and click the 'New Performance' button. Our step-by-step conductor guide will walk you through setting your venue, timing, and ticketing tiers.
What is the ticket refund policy? expand_more
Refunds are handled according to the specific event organizer's policy. Generally, requests made 7 days prior to the event are automatically processed through the platform.
Is my account security guaranteed? expand_more
We use multi-factor authentication (MFA) and biometric verification hooks for high-value transactions to ensure your credentials remain in your hands only.
Can I host private or invitation-only events? expand_more
Yes. When creating an event, you can toggle the 'Visibility' setting to 'Unlisted' and distribute unique access keys to your selected audience.
What are the platform fees for organizers? expand_more
We charge a flat 2.5% orchestration fee per paid ticket. Free events are always hosted without charge to the organizer.
How do I contact a specific event organizer? expand_more
On every event page, there is a 'Message Organizer' button which opens a direct secure channel for your queries.
